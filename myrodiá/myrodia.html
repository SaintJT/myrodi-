About Us

Welcome to @MYRODIÁ, where fragrance meets the essence of Florence's floral beauty. Nestled in the heart of the enchanting gardens of Florence, Italy, our perfumes are crafted with passion and inspired by the captivating scents that surround us. At @MYRODIÁ, we believe in the transformative power of fragrance. Each bottle encapsulates the spirit of our beloved city, renowned for its rich history, artistic heritage, and, of course, its breathtaking gardens filled with an abundance of blooming flowers. Our journey began amidst the lush landscapes and vibrant colors of Florence's botanical treasures. Inspired by the timeless elegance and natural allure of these gardens, we embarked on a quest to capture their essence in every scent we create. Every fragrance we offer is a labor of love, meticulously crafted by skilled perfumers using only the finest ingredients sourced from around the world. From the delicate notes of Tuscan roses to the intoxicating aroma of jasmine blossoms, each perfume tells a story and evokes emotions that transport you to the serene beauty of our floral haven. But @MYRODIÁ is more than just a collection of exquisite fragrances. We are a celebration of craftsmanship, authenticity, and the boundless wonders of nature. With every spritz, we invite you to experience the romance and splendor of Florence, enveloping your senses in a bouquet of unforgettable scents. Join us on a sensory journey through the enchanting gardens of Florence and discover the magic of @MYRODIÁ. Let our perfumes be your olfactory passport to the timeless allure of Italy's most cherished landscapes. Indulge in the essence of Florence. Indulge in @MYRODIÁ.
